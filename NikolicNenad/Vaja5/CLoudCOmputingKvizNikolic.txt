1. Which of the following is an example of virtualization?

   Pravilen odgovor je d) Multiple virtual machines running on the same server. Namen virtualizacije da na enem fizičnem strežniku lahko poganjamo več različnih virtualizacij.


2. Which resources provide the ‘brains’ to process your workloads, including what is required to process and run requests from applications?

   Pravilen odgovor je a) Compute. Za izvajanje procesov potrebujemo računske vire, kot sta CPU in RAM, ki omogočajo obdelavo podatkov ter izvajanje aplikacij in njihovih zahtevkov.		


3. When you need to provision a resource within the cloud, _________ makes it almost immediately available to you to allocate where and when you need it.

   Pravilen odgovor je d) on-demand resourcing. V oblaku lahko resource dobiš takoj ni jih potrebno čakati.
   

4.A ________ cloud deployment model is where a vendor makes available the use of shared infrastructure, including compute, storage and network resources, that can be provisioned on demand and typically accessed over the internet.
 
   Pravilen odgovor je d) public. V javnem oblaku ponudnik omogoča uporabo deljene infrastrukture (compute, storage, network), ki je dostopna preko interneta.
   

5.How are costs generally charged for cloud resources?

  Pravilen odgovor je a) Utility-based metering where you only pay for what you use. V oblaku plačaš samo to, kar porabiš.

10.Which cloud resource saves and retains data, and is available at an almost unlimited amount within the cloud?
   
    Pravilen odgovor je d) Storage. Storage omogoča shranjevanje in ohranjanje podatkov v oblaku.


11.The ___________________ cloud service model offers the greatest amount of user customization and control but requires the greatest amount of user administration including the operating system, but not physical hardware.
   
   Pravilen odgovor je a) Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS). Pri IaaS uporabniku upravlja z infrastrukturo.


12.What benefit of cloud computing allows you to increase or decrease the power or quantity of resources at any time, without extensive installation or physical processes of provisioning?
   
   Pravilen odgovor je b) Scalability. Scalability omogoča povečanje ali zmanjšanje količine virov glede na potrebe.

15.The ___________________ model requires no software installation and focuses on design simplicity and ease of use. It offers a minimal level of service customization.

   Pravilen odgovor je b) Software-as-a-Service. Aplikacijo uporabljaš preko brskalnika.

16.You are leading a team of developers on a project whose goal is to improve customer experience on a credit card company's website.
The solution requires that the developers write business requirement-oriented custom code. Your client wants the developers not to have the overhead of managing operating systems, runtimes, and middleware.
   
   Pravilen odgovor je b) Platform as a Service (PaaS). Ker razvijalci pišejo kodo, in ne skrbijo za infrastrukturo.
